# ABOUT

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at University College Dublin. I completed my PhD at Mokpo National University, South Korea, where I also received both my Bachelors and Masters Degrees in Multimedia Engineering. My research interests include, but not limited to, trustworthy AI, multimodal deep learning, affective computing, and healthcare AI.

  • Healthcare AI: Medical Image Analysis, Survival Analysis, AI for Mental Health
